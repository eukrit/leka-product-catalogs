Splashpad® and aquatic attractions for municipal and commercial projects

At Vortex, we know water is more than fun, it’s a catalyst for connection, creativity, and community. That’s why every Vortex solution, powered by innovative water play equipment, turns a splash into something bigger. Whether it’s a neighborhood Splashpad or a jaw-dropping waterslide, we design experiences that help kids grow, bring people together, and boost business. The fun may dry up, but the impact lasts.

About us

Inspiration

Every bold idea starts with a splash of imagination. At Vortex, we’re inspired by the world in motion, from flowing rivers to soaring skylines to the simple joy of play. We turn these ideas into innovative water play equipment through expert aquatic play design, creating spaces that spark wonder, build bonds, and unleash creativity for all ages.

Nature's Lush Greenery

Leafs 

Straight from our Watergarden collection, Leafs bring the beauty of the outdoors to your Splashpad®. With flowing forms and vibrant shapes, these organic water features echo the feel of lush greenery and wild landscapes. For curious kids, it’s not just play, it’s a rainforest to explore, a jungle to conquer, a pond to splash through. It’s nature, reimagined through water play solutions that spark imagination and connection.

A Deep Sea Adventure

A deep sea adventure

Dive into discovery with the Sea Silhouette collection, a nautically themed lineup that turns your Splashpad® into an underwater world. These bold, elevated products are scaled for big-time play and serve as imaginative anchors for the whole space. With just the right touch of abstraction, they invite kids to dream up sea creatures, sunken treasure, and oceanic quests. It’s immersive fun, powered by our signature themed Splashpad solutions.

Rain, Rain Come Today

Rain, rain come today.

What kid doesn’t love a good splash in the rain? Inspired by that universal joy, Vortex designed Ombrellos, the ultimate rainy-day play feature. Part of our Splashpad® Contemporary collection, these sleek, urban water park accessories bring stylish fun to any landscape. Whether standing solo or woven into a larger design, Ombrellos turn every drop into a chance for sensory-rich, timeless play.

Explore Through Movement

Explore Through Movement

The Explora Collection turns curiosity into motion. With bright, colorful orbs, some oversized for extra impact, kids are invited to push, pull, and spin their way through a world of play. These kinetic products aren’t just fun; they’re smart. Little hands, big discoveries. Pico and Alto, two new Water Journey™ experiences, bring the wonder of nature to early childhood play. Commercial Water Slides Built for Thrill


Big Fun, Bold Drops
Vortex Aquatic Structures takes water fun to the next level with commercial water slides designed for every kind of adventurer. From gentle curves for first-time riders to heart-pounding high-intensity drops, each slide is fully customizable and engineered for excitement. Whether it stands alone or integrates with your Elevations™ or Splashpad® structure, it’s a water park solution built to thrill, and built to last.

Wellness Through Water and Design


CoolHub™: Where Calm Meets Community
CoolHub™ isn’t just about cooling off, it’s about slowing down. This wellness-focused solution blends, soft rain, mist and seating into a serene escape for parks, plazas, and public spaces. Thoughtfully designed for comfort and inclusivity, CoolHub™ creates space for rest, relief, and reconnection, proof that wellness can flow as easily as water.

FAQ

What are some examples of Vortex installations to date?

With over 10,000 projects in 50 countries, there’s lots to choose from! You can find a selection of stories about municipal and commercial projects – from across the globe - here.

How do I place an order for a Vortex Splash pad?

Depending on your geographic location, you will work with a Vortex sales executive or local channel partner. They will meet with you to discuss your community needs, and work towards the creation of a splash pad that is right for you. Find your closest Vortex representative here.

Does Vortex offer financing options?

Vortex does not offer financing options. However, your Vortex sales executive or local channel partner will have suggestions to assist you with the fundraising and eventual purchase of your Splashpad. Find your closest Vortex representative here.

Is there a gallery of Vortex’s most recent aquatic structures?

Yes, we have a page dedicated to recent project stories here.

Can you explain more about Vortex features?

Vortex continues to innovate with unique designs and sustainable water management and aquatic filtration systems. Our colorful open-ended designs invite children to fill in the blanks and create playful environments from their own imaginations. And every Vortex solution is bolstered by smart technology that enhances play experiences, elevates safety, and makes the expansion of your facility easier and more cost-effective. Learn more about our expertise. 

What ages do Vortex water attractions cater to?

Vortex Splashpads® are designed for children of all ages and abilities. We can create toddler zones for the little ones right up to bigger splash events for teens. Discover how splash pads are designed here.

How do I stay updated about Vortex?

We encourage you to follow us on Facebook, LinkedIn, or Instagram to learn about our latest water innovations, projects, awards, and company news.
